## Key Features

### 1. AI Task Prioritization
Intelligent algorithms analyze project complexity, team capacity, and individual strengths to automatically rank and assign tasks. Save hours of manual planning and ensure your most critical work always gets the attention it deserves.

### 2. Real-Time Team Sync
Instant updates and context-aware notifications keep your distributed team perfectly aligned. See changes, communicate seamlessly, and maintain team momentum without endless status meetings or email chains.

### 3. Smart Deadline Management
Our AI predicts potential delays, suggests optimal timelines, and automatically adjusts project schedules. Proactively identify risks, redistribute workloads, and keep projects on track with predictive insights that traditional tools can't match.

### 4. Powerful Integrations
Connect FlowSync with your favorite tools—Slack, GitHub, Zoom, and more. Our robust API and one-click integrations create a unified workflow, eliminating app-switching and ensuring your entire tech stack works in harmony.
