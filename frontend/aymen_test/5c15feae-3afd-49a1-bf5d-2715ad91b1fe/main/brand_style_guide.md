# Brand Style Guide: IronPulse Gym

## 1. Visual Identity & Concept
IronPulse is designed to evoke strength, motivation, and modern fitness. The identity balances high-energy accents with professional, deep neutrals to create a "premium athletic" feel.

## 2. Color Palette
The palette uses a high-contrast approach to ensure visibility and energy.

- **Primary (Action):** `#3d87cc` (Steel Blue) - Trust and stability.
- **Secondary:** `#b75088` (Deep Magenta) - Energy and passion.
- **Accent (High-Vis):** `#80cc20` (Electric Lime) - Motivation and growth.
- **Neutrals:**
  - Darkest: `#1e1e1e` (Deep Charcoal) - Base for strength.
  - Mid: `#8a8b8c` (Slate) - Balance.
  - Lightest: `#f4f7f9` (Off-White) - Cleanliness and space.

**Contrast Compliance (WCAG AA):**
- Electric Lime on Deep Charcoal: 8.4:1 (Pass)
- Deep Charcoal on Off-White: 15.5:1 (Pass)
- Steel Blue on Deep Charcoal: 4.39:1 (Pass for Large Text)

## 3. Typography
**Font Family:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings:** Bold, tight letter-spacing (-0.02em) to convey power.
- **Body:** Regular weight, high readability for instructional content.

**Type Scale:**
- Display: 3.5rem
- H1: 2.5rem
- H2: 2rem
- Body: 1rem
- Caption: 0.875rem

## 4. Logo Usage
- **Primary Logo:** Full wordmark with icon.
- **Icon Only:** Used for social media and app favicons.
- **Monochrome:** For print and high-contrast environments.
- **Placement:** Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the 'I' in IronPulse.
