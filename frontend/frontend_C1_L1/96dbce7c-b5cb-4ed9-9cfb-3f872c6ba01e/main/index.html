Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about DataPulse security, integrations, onboarding, and enterprise capabilities.

How does DataPulse connect to my existing data sources?

DataPulse offers 50+ native integrations with popular tools like Salesforce, Snowflake, Google Analytics, Stripe, HubSpot, and PostgreSQL. For proprietary systems, our open REST API and Python SDK allow your engineering team to push data directly. Most connections are configured in under five minutes using our secure OAuth-based authentication flow.

Is my data secure and compliant with industry regulations?

Absolutely. DataPulse is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit via TLS 1.3. We offer granular role-based access controls, IP whitelisting, SSO via SAML 2.0, and detailed audit logs. For regulated industries, our Enterprise tier supports on-premise deployment so your data never leaves your infrastructure.

How long does onboarding typically take?

Most teams are up and running within one business day. Our guided setup wizard walks you through connecting your first data source, building your initial dashboard, and inviting team members. Pro and Enterprise customers receive a dedicated onboarding specialist who ensures your first report is live within 48 hours. We also offer live training sessions and a comprehensive video library.

Can I export reports and share them with external stakeholders?

Yes. You can export any dashboard or report as a PDF, PNG, or CSV with a single click. Scheduled email reports can be sent daily, weekly, or monthly to anyone — even recipients without a DataPulse account. For external partners, you can generate secure, read-only share links with optional password protection and expiration dates.

Do you offer custom machine learning models for Enterprise clients?

Enterprise customers can request custom ML models tailored to their specific business logic and data structures. Our data science team works with you to define the problem, engineer features, train the model, and deploy it directly into your DataPulse environment. Typical turnaround is 2–4 weeks depending on complexity, and all models include ongoing monitoring and retraining.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

We never throttle your data or hide critical insights. If you approach a limit — such as dashboard count or user seats — we notify you immediately and offer a prorated upgrade. Your existing dashboards and reports remain fully accessible during any transition. There are no surprise overage fees.
