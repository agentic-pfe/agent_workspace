## Product Description
### Origin & Craft
Summit Roast sources exclusively from high-altitude farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala, where elevation, soil, and microclimate converge to create exceptional coffee. Our beans are hand-selected by local farmers who have cultivated these lands for generations.

### Roast Profile
- **Roast Levels**: Light to Medium
- **Flavor Spectrum**: Bright, Complex, Nuanced
- **Tasting Notes**: 
  - Colombian Peaks: Caramel, Toasted Almond, Bright Citrus
  - Ethiopian Highlands: Jasmine, Bergamot, Blueberry
  - Guatemalan Slopes: Dark Chocolate, Honey, Subtle Spice

## Key Benefits
1. **Small-Batch Precision**
   Roasted in limited quantities to preserve each bean's unique character. No mass production, just pure craft.

2. **Ethical Sourcing**
   Direct trade relationships ensure fair compensation for farmers and sustainable agricultural practices.

3. **Altitude-Driven Quality**
   Beans grown above 1,500 meters develop more complex sugars and intense flavors.

4. **Carbon-Neutral Commitment**
   We offset our entire production cycle, making every cup environmentally responsible.
