Our curriculum is designed around five foundational pillars that give you both conceptual understanding and practical capability. Each outcome is tied to a real-world project so you graduate with demonstrable skills, not just theoretical knowledge.

AI Fundamentals & History

Understand what AI really is — and isn't. Explore the history from Turing to transformers, distinguish between narrow AI, general AI, and superintelligence, and learn how modern AI systems actually work under the hood.

Machine Learning Workflows

Build and train your first ML models using no-code platforms and beginner-friendly Python. Master supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and the complete data pipeline from collection to deployment.

Natural Language Processing

Computer Vision Basics

Enable machines to see and interpret the visual world. Create image classifiers, facial recognition models, and object detection systems using pre-trained models and transfer learning.

Capstone Portfolio Project

Apply everything you've learned to a real-world AI project of your choice. Receive personalized feedback and graduate with a polished portfolio piece to share with employers or clients.
