## Content Sections

### Section 1: Lessons — Standards-Aligned Science Units

**Heading:** Comprehensive Lessons That Follow NGSS Standards

**Body:**
Our lesson library spans all core middle school science domains: Life Science, Physical Science, Earth & Space Science, and Engineering Design. Each unit includes step-by-step lesson plans, printable student handouts, embedded vocabulary support, and formative assessment checkpoints. Every lesson is tagged by grade level (6–8), topic, and NGSS performance expectation, so you can find exactly what you need in seconds. Lessons are designed for 45–60 minute class periods and include differentiation strategies for English learners, students with IEPs, and advanced learners.

### Section 2: Interactive Activities — Learn by Doing

**Heading:** Hands-On Simulations and Virtual Labs

**Body:**
Give your students the power to experiment, fail safely, and try again. Our interactive activities include browser-based simulations where students can build circuits, model ecosystems, explore chemical reactions, and simulate plate tectonics — all with zero setup or cleanup. Each activity comes with a guided inquiry worksheet and reflection prompts that encourage students to think like real scientists. Compatible with Chromebooks, tablets, and interactive whiteboards.

**Image Prompt:**
A split-screen digital illustration showing two middle school students working on a tablet outdoors, with a virtual ecosystem simulation displayed on the screen showing plants, animals, and a food web. On the right side, a clean, modern laptop displays a colorful interactive circuit-building interface with wires, bulbs, and switches. Bright, optimistic, tech-forward aesthetic. Digital art style.

### Section 3: Teacher Resources — Everything You Need to Teach Confidently

**Heading:** Save Time with Ready-to-Use Classroom Tools

**Body:**
We know your time is precious. The Teacher Resources hub gives you editable lesson templates, customizable rubrics, quiz banks with auto-grading options, lab safety checklists, parent communication letters in English and Spanish, and pacing guides for full-year and trimester schedules. ### Section 4: About Us — Our Mission and Team

**Heading:** Built by Educators, for Educators

**Body:**
ScienceQuest was founded by a team of middle school science teachers, curriculum specialists, and educational technology designers who believed that every student deserves access to inspiring, rigorous science education. We are a nonprofit initiative supported by the National Science Teaching Association and partnered with university STEM outreach programs. Our mission is to reduce teacher burnout by providing high-quality, ready-to-teach resources — and to spark lasting scientific curiosity in every middle school student.

They are smiling and pointing at the board, holding markers and tablets. Casual, approachable professional setting with plants and bookshelves. Bright, genuine, community-focused atmosphere. Photorealistic style.
