Crafted at Altitude, Born of Purpose

At Summit Roast, we believe the best coffee grows where the air is thin and the soil is rich. That's why we partner directly with smallholder farmers perched above 1,500 meters in the world's finest coffee-growing regions. Every bean is hand-picked, sun-dried, and small-batch roasted in our Portland roastery within 48 hours of arrival. The result? A cup that's not just delicious — it's intentional. Bright acidity, deep cocoa undertones, and a finish as smooth as the mountain mist.

Why Summit Roast

Single-Origin. Always.

Every bag is traceable to a specific farm and harvest — no blends, no mystery. You'll know the region, elevation, processing method, and tasting notes for every bean you brew.

Roasted to Order

We never roast until you order. Your coffee hits the roaster within hours of purchase and ships the same day — arriving at your door at peak freshness, typically within 3 days.

Direct Trade & Fair Pay

30–50% above Fair Trade pricing. We meet our farmers face-to-face, invest in their communities, and fund reforestation. Full transparency on every bag.

100% Compostable Packaging

From the outer bag to the one-way valve — everything is made from plant-based materials. No plastic, no waste, no compromise.

Brew Guides Included

Each shipment includes a customized brewing guide with water temperature, grind size, and ratios dialed in for your specific bean and preferred brew method.

Small-Batch Craft

We roast in 25-pound batches in a converted 1940s cast-iron roaster. Each batch is cupped and approved before it ever reaches your doorstep.

Flavor Notes Worth Waking Up For

Our rotating single-origin lineup offers something for every palate. From the bright, citrus-forward Ethiopia Yirgacheffe (think blueberry and jasmine) to the deep, chocolatey Colombia Huila (brown sugar, red apple, silky body) and the nutty, balanced Peru Cajamarca (caramel, almond, hint of honey). Every bag is roasted to order and shipped within 24 hours — so your coffee is always peak-fresh. Tasting notes, roast level, and altitude are printed on every bag, because you deserve to know exactly what you're brewing.

Ethical Sourcing Is Not Optional

We pay 30–50% above Fair Trade pricing directly to our farming partners. Every relationship is built on transparency: we visit each farm annually, invest in community water projects, and fund reforestation initiatives that have planted over 10,000 native trees in coffee-growing regions. Our packaging is 100% compostable, and our roastery runs on renewable energy. When you brew Summit Roast, you're supporting a supply chain that puts people and planet first — no exceptions.
