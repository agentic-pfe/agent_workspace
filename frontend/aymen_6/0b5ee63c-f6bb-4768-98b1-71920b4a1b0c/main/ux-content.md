## 3-Benefit Breakdown

### 1. Joint Recovery — Move Without Limits

High-impact training strains cartilage and connective tissue. The omega-3 EPA in AquaPure helps reduce exercise-induced inflammation and supports synovial fluid health, so your joints stay lubricated and resilient. Athletes report noticeably less stiffness and faster return-to-training after heavy sessions.

- **Image Prompt:** An athlete stretching after a run in a sunlit gym with foam rollers and yoga mat visible. Soft focus on knees and joints, warm amber and teal lighting. Motion and recovery theme, lifestyle fitness photography. *(Photo)*

### 2. Brain Function — Stay Locked In

Mental fatigue can sabotage performance as much as physical exhaustion. DHA, the structural fat of the brain, sharpens reaction time, focus, and decision-making under pressure. AquaPure provides a potent DHA dose to keep your mind as agile as your body — from early morning training to late-night recovery.

- **Image Prompt:** A focused athlete in a clean modern space, reviewing a training plan on a tablet, with a glass of water and the AquaPure bottle on a wooden desk. Natural window light, blue-toned background suggesting calm concentration. Lifestyle photography. *(Photo)*

### 3. Heart Health — The Endurance Advantage

A strong heart is your most important muscle. Omega-3s support healthy blood pressure, improve circulation, and reduce exercise-induced cardiac strain. For endurance athletes, better cardiovascular efficiency means sustained power output over longer distances. AquaPure helps you go further, longer.

- **Image Prompt:** An athlete running along a coastal path at golden hour, ocean waves in the background, wearing a heart rate monitor. Wide-angle shot conveying movement, freedom, and cardiovascular health. Warm golden light, inspirational fitness photography. *(Photo)*
