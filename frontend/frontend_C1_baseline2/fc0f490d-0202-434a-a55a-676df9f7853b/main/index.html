About Me

I'm Alex — a UX designer with 8+ years of experience turning complex problems into elegant, user-centered solutions. I believe the best designs come from deep listening: to users, to data, and to the teams who bring products to life. When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me mentoring junior designers, writing about design systems, or experimenting with creative code.

My approach is rooted in research, shaped by collaboration, and refined through relentless iteration. Every project is a chance to learn something new about how people think, feel, and behave.
