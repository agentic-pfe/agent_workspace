Our Story

From a garage to a global creative force

Forma Studio was founded in 2018 by a small group of designers who believed that great design could change the world. What started as late-night sketching sessions in a cramped garage has grown into a full-service digital agency serving clients across six continents.

We've helped startups find their voice, guided established brands through digital transformations, and launched products that millions of people use every day. Our approach blends artistic vision with technical precision — every pixel, every line of code, every interaction is crafted with intention.

Today, our team of twelve spans three time zones, united by a shared obsession with quality and a belief that the best work happens when creativity and technology dance together.
